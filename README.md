# Telecom-Customer-Churn-Analysis-Power-BI
Project Title: Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Description:
Developed an interactive dashboard to analyze customer churn behavior in the telecom industry. Compared active and churned customers to identify key factors influencing retention, including demographics, contract type, services, and payment methods. Generated insights to support data-driven decision-making.

Tasks:

Cleaned and transformed telecom dataset for accurate analysis
Analyzed customer demographics, tenure, and service usage patterns
Performed churn vs non-churn comparison to identify trends
Created KPI metrics such as total customers, churn count, and average charges
Designed interactive dashboards with filters and drill-down features
Identified high-risk customer segments likely to churn

Features:

Business Problem:

High customer churn leading to revenue loss and reduced customer lifetime value

Key Questions Addressed:

Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
How do contract types impact customer retention?
What role do services (Internet, Phone) play in churn behavior?
How do payment methods and monthly charges influence churn?
What is the relationship between customer tenure and churn rate?

Business Impact:

Helped identify high-risk customers for targeted retention strategies
Provided insights to improve contract offerings and pricing models
Enabled data-driven decision-making to reduce churn rate
Improved understanding of customer behavior and service usage patterns

Tech Stack:
Power BI, Excel, DAX

Data Source:
Telecom Customer Dataset (IBM Sample Dataset / Kaggle)
